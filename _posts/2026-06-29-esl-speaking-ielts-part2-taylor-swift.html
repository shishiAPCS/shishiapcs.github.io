Section 1: Basic Introduction

1. Basic Introduction

Useful facts / 基本信息 IELTS-friendly phrases / 可借用英文表达 Chinese Meaning / 中文意思
Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter known for country, pop, folk-inspired, and synth-pop music. Taylor Swift 是美国创作型歌手，作品横跨乡村、流行、民谣风和合成器流行等风格。 a genre-crossing singer-songwriter 跨越多种音乐风格的创作型歌手
She was born Taylor Alison Swift on December 13, 1989, in Reading, Pennsylvania. 她的全名是 Taylor Alison Swift，1989 年 12 月 13 日出生于美国宾夕法尼亚州 Reading。 be born and raised in 出生并成长于
She became famous first as a young country artist and later became one of the biggest pop stars in the world. 她最初以年轻乡村歌手身份走红，后来成长为全球最有影响力的流行歌手之一。 rise from country music to global pop 从乡村音乐走向全球流行乐
She is known not only for singing, but also for songwriting, storytelling, business decisions, tours, films, and fan culture. 她不只是会唱歌，还和创作、讲故事、商业决策、巡演、电影和粉丝文化紧密相关。 more than just a singer 不只是歌手
Her official website shares music, news, tour information, and official merchandise. 她的官方网站会发布音乐、新闻、巡演信息和官方周边商品。 stay connected with fans 和粉丝保持连接

3. Songwriting and Storytelling

Useful facts / 基本信息 IELTS-friendly phrases / 可借用英文表达 Chinese Meaning / 中文意思
Swift is especially known for writing personal, detailed songs about emotions, memories, relationships, growing up, and public life. Swift 尤其擅长写个人化、细节感强的歌曲，内容涉及情绪、回忆、关系、成长和公众生活。 write from personal experience 从个人经历出发创作
Many fans like her songs because they feel like short stories set to music. 很多粉丝喜欢她的歌，是因为这些歌听起来像配上音乐的短故事。 turn feelings into stories 把感受变成故事
Her songs often use simple language, but the images and emotions are easy to remember. 她的歌词语言常常并不复杂，但画面感和情绪很容易让人记住。 make a lasting impression 留下持久印象
She has submitted songs such as All Too Well, Blank Space, Anti-Hero, Love Story, and The Last Great American Dynasty for Songwriters Hall of Fame consideration. 她曾提交 All Too Well、Blank Space、Anti-Hero、Love Story 和 The Last Great American Dynasty 等歌曲用于 Songwriters Hall of Fame 评审。 be recognized for songwriting 因词曲创作受到认可

4. Career Breakthrough and Awards

Useful facts / 基本信息 IELTS-friendly phrases / 可借用英文表达 Chinese Meaning / 中文意思

Fearless helped her reach a much wider audience, with songs such as Love Story and You Belong With Me. 《Fearless》帮助她触达更广泛的听众，其中包括 Love Story 和 You Belong With Me 等歌曲。 reach a wider audience 触达更广泛的受众

Section 2: IELTS Part 2 Topics

A. Strong Fit Topics

Chinese Topic Name English Topic Name How to Use / 如何使用
遇到困难终成功的人 Describe a person who met difficulties but succeeded Use Swift as a person who faced criticism, public pressure, and the master-recordings dispute but kept creating music. Focus on resilience, the Taylor’s Version project, and how she turned a setback into a stronger career. 可以讲 Swift 面对批评、公众压力和母带版权争议后仍然继续创作。重点讲抗压能力、Taylor’s Version 重录计划，以及她如何把挫折变成更强的事业动力。
成功的生意人 Describe a person you know who has a successful business Swift is a strong example because she is not only a singer but also a powerful business figure. Talk about her albums, tours, film releases, merchandise, and control of her own brand. Swift 很适合这个题，因为她不只是歌手，也是很强的商业人物。可以讲她的专辑、巡演、电影发行、周边商品，以及对个人品牌的掌控。
长久目标 Describe a long-term goal you would like to achieve Use her as inspiration. Say your long-term goal is to become good at storytelling, music, writing, business, or creative work, and Swift shows how small daily practice can become long-term success. 可以把她作为灵感来源。你的长期目标可以是提升讲故事、音乐、写作、商业或创作能力，而 Swift 说明日常练习可以慢慢变成长期成功。
长远志向 Describe an ambition that you have had for a long time Connect the topic to her childhood dream of becoming a songwriter and performer. Then shift to your own ambition and explain how her persistence makes the goal feel possible. 把题目和她从小想成为创作型歌手、表演者的梦想联系起来，再转到你自己的志向，说明她的坚持让这个目标显得更可实现。
名人出演的广告 Describe an advertisement with a famous person in it Use a Taylor Swift brand campaign, album promotion, or streaming-platform promotion. Focus on why her image works: she has a huge fan base, a clear style, and strong public trust. 可以讲 Taylor Swift 的品牌广告、专辑宣传或流媒体平台宣传。重点讲她为什么适合广告：粉丝群体大、风格清楚、公众信任度高。
最近看过的电视/网络节目 Describe a TV show/online program you have watched recently Use a documentary, interview, or Disney+ program about The Eras Tour. Explain what it shows: performance, planning, teamwork, fans, and the pressure behind a huge tour. 可以讲关于 The Eras Tour 的纪录片、采访或 Disney+ 节目。说明它展示了表演、规划、团队合作、粉丝，以及大型巡演背后的压力。
有趣视频 Describe an interesting video Choose a music video, live performance clip, interview, or behind-the-scenes video. The easiest angle is a video that shows how she turns a simple song idea into a complete visual story. 选择她的 MV、现场片段、采访或幕后视频。最容易的角度是：这个视频展示了她如何把一个简单的歌曲想法变成完整的视觉故事。
快乐活动 Describe a time when you organized a happy event successfully Describe organizing a Taylor Swift-themed birthday party, English-song sharing event, or class activity. Use her songs, friendship bracelets, costumes, and lyrics to make the event fun. 可以讲组织一次 Taylor Swift 主题生日会、英文歌分享会或班级活动。用她的歌、friendship bracelets、服装和歌词让活动更有趣。
重要决定 Describe an important decision that you made Use Swift’s decision to re-record her albums as a model. Then talk about your own decision, such as protecting your work, choosing a creative path, or learning English through music. 可以把她重录专辑的决定作为参照，再讲你自己的重要决定，比如保护自己的作品、选择创作方向，或通过音乐学英语。
在团队中工作 Describe a time when you worked in a group Use The Eras Tour as a lead-in: a huge show needs dancers, musicians, technicians, designers, and managers. Then describe your own group project and how clear roles helped. 可以用 The Eras Tour 引入：大型演出需要舞者、乐手、技术人员、设计师和管理人员。然后讲你自己的小组项目，突出分工明确。
想见的名人 Describe a famous person you would like to meet This is one of the most natural topics. Say you would like to meet Swift to ask about songwriting, handling pressure, planning a career, and staying creative for many years. 这是最自然的话题之一。可以说你想见 Swift，问她关于写歌、抗压、职业规划，以及如何多年保持创造力的问题。
近期观影 Describe a movie you watched and enjoyed recently Use Taylor Swift | The Eras Tour or another concert film related to her. Focus on the music, stage design, audience energy, and why it felt different from a normal movie. 可以用《Taylor Swift | The Eras Tour》或其他相关演唱会电影。重点讲音乐、舞台设计、观众氛围，以及它为什么不像普通电影。
机智解决问题的人 Describe a person who solved a problem in a smart way Talk about how Swift responded to the masters dispute by re-recording her earlier albums. It is a smart solution because it used creativity, patience, and fan support instead of only legal arguments. 可以讲 Swift 如何用重录早期专辑来回应母带版权争议。这个办法聪明在于，它不是只靠法律争论，而是结合了创作能力、耐心和粉丝支持。
常做计划的人 Describe a person who makes plans a lot Use Swift as a planner: album eras, release timing, visual clues, tour design, and fan communication all show careful long-term planning. 可以把 Swift 讲成善于规划的人：专辑 era、发行节奏、视觉线索、巡演设计和粉丝沟通都体现了长期规划。

B. Usable Bridge Topics

Chinese Topic Name English Topic Name How to Use / 如何使用
在成功公司工作的人 Describe a person who works in a successful company This is a bridge topic. Do not call her an office worker.
