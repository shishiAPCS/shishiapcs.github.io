# Attending a Major Skateboarding Competition

# 去现场看一场大型滑板比赛

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
I would love to attend a major skateboarding competition because I admire the athletes’ skill and want to enjoy the exciting atmosphere with my family and friends.
我很想去现场看一场大型滑板比赛，因为我很佩服运动员的技术，也想和家人朋友一起感受那种激动人心的氛围。

**Best for these topics:**

* a sports event you would like to attend
* an exciting event
* a memorable experience you want to have
* something you want to do with family or friends
* a sport you are interested in
* an event that would make you feel excited

**Emotion path:**
interested → excited → respectful → realistic → thrilled → connected

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Event:** a major skateboarding competition
（事件：一场大型滑板比赛）

**Reason for interest:** wanted to attend for years
（感兴趣的原因：想去很多年了）

**Problem:** not common where I live
（现实困难：我住的地方不常见）

**People:** family and close friends
（一起去的人：家人和亲近的朋友）

**What attracts me:** skill, difficulty, atmosphere
（吸引我的地方：技巧、难度、现场氛围）

**Personal feeling:** admire athletes but don’t want to take the injury risk myself
（个人感受：佩服运动员，但自己不想冒受伤风险）

**Expected result:** adrenaline rush + stress relief + shared memory
（期待结果：兴奋感 + 释放压力 + 共同回忆）

## C. Must-know chunks

## 必备表达

* **I’d absolutely love to…**
  我特别想…… / 我非常想……

* **I’ve wanted to do this for years**
  我已经想做这件事很多年了

* **I just haven’t had the chance yet**
  只是一直还没有机会

* **where I live**
  在我住的地方

* **events like this aren’t very common**
  这样的活动并不常见

* **perform live**
  现场表演 / 现场展示

* **a couple of close friends**
  几个亲近的朋友

* **make the experience more fun and memorable**
  让这次经历更有趣、更难忘

* **what fascinates me most is…**
  最吸引我的是……

* **make it look easy (idiomatic)**
  看起来很简单，但其实不简单

* **in reality**
  实际上 / 事实上

* **an incredible amount of practice**
  大量的练习

* **physically demanding**
  对体力要求很高的

* **at this stage of my life**
  在我人生的这个阶段 / 现在这个年纪

* **be aware of the risk of injuries**
  意识到受伤的风险

* **instead of doing it myself**
  与其自己去做 / 不自己上场，而是……

* **be in the crowd**
  在观众席里 / 在人群中观看

* **cheer for the athletes**
  为运动员加油

* **enjoy the atmosphere**
  享受现场氛围

* **give me a real adrenaline rush (idiomatic)**
  让我感到非常兴奋、热血沸腾

* **forget about work stress for a while**
  暂时忘掉工作压力

* **once-in-a-lifetime experience (idiomatic)**
  一生难得一次的经历

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

A sports event I’d **absolutely love to attend** is a major skateboarding competition. I’ve actually wanted to go to one for years, but I just **haven’t had the chance yet**. The main reason is that where I live, events like this aren’t very common, so it’s pretty hard to see professional skateboarders **perform live**.
我特别想去现场看的一类体育赛事，是大型滑板比赛。其实我已经想去很多年了，只是一直还没有机会。主要原因是，在我住的地方，这类比赛并不常见，所以很难现场看到职业滑板运动员表演。

If I could go, I’d probably bring my family and **a couple of close friends**. I think watching something exciting together always makes the experience more fun and memorable.
如果我能去的话，我大概会带上家人，再叫上几个比较亲近的朋友。我觉得大家一起看一场很刺激的比赛，会让这段经历更有趣，也更难忘。

What fascinates me most about skateboarding is how challenging it is. Professional skateboarders **make it look easy**, but in reality, every trick takes **an incredible amount of practice**. Some moves are also **physically demanding**, which is one of the reasons I really respect athletes like them.
滑板最吸引我的地方，是它真的很有挑战性。职业滑板选手会让那些动作看起来很简单，但实际上，每一个动作背后都需要大量练习。有些动作对身体素质要求也很高，这也是我特别尊重这些运动员的原因之一。

To be honest, I don’t think I’m physically strong enough to do those kinds of tricks myself. And **at this stage of my life**, I’m a little more aware of the **risk of injuries**.
说实话，我不觉得自己的身体素质足够好，可以去做那些动作。而且到了现在这个阶段，我也更清楚受伤的风险。

So instead of doing it myself, I’d rather **be in the crowd**, **cheering for the athletes** and enjoying the atmosphere. I think being there would give me a real **adrenaline rush**, and it would also be a great way to forget about work stress for a while.
所以与其自己上场尝试，我更愿意站在观众席里，为运动员加油，享受现场氛围。我觉得在现场一定会让我非常兴奋，也会是一个暂时忘掉工作压力的好方式。

I’d probably feel thrilled, inspired, and full of energy after the event. I also think it would bring me closer to my family and friends because we’d be sharing such a unique experience—maybe even a **once-in-a-lifetime experience**—together.
比赛结束后，我应该会觉得特别激动、很受鼓舞，也充满能量。我也觉得这会让我和家人朋友更亲近，因为我们一起经历了一件非常特别的事情，甚至可能是一生难得一次的经历。
