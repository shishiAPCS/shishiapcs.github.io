# Admiring Tony Hawk's Skateboarding Legacy

# 我钦佩的滑板传奇 Tony Hawk

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览-

**One-line summary:**
Tony Hawk is a legendary skateboarder whose long career and business vision make him truly admirable.
Tony Hawk 是一位传奇滑板运动员，他的长期坚持和商业眼光让人非常钦佩。

**Best for these topics:**

* a sportsperson you admire
* a famous person you like
* a person who is successful
* a person who influenced you
* a role model

**Emotion path:**
unaware → curious → interested → impressed → inspired

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

To be honest, one of the most successful sportspeople I admire is **Tony Hawk**, who is **widely known as one of the greatest skateboarders in the world**.
说实话，我最钦佩的运动员之一是 **Tony Hawk**，他被**广泛认为是世界上最伟大的滑板选手之一**。

I first **came across his name** when I was in elementary school, mainly through his **video game series, Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater**. At that time, I only knew the game, and I didn’t even realize that Tony Hawk was a real person.
我第一次**听说他的名字**是在小学的时候，主要是通过他的**滑板游戏系列《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》**。那时候我只知道这个游戏，甚至都不知道 Tony Hawk 是一个真实存在的人。

Later, when I was in high school, I started to **get into skateboarding myself**. I began watching videos and doing a lot of research, and that’s when I came across his name again.
后来到了高中，我开始**自己玩滑板**。我开始看各种视频、查资料，也就是那个时候，我再次接触到他的名字。

Before I knew it, I had **fallen down a rabbit hole** learning everything about him.
不知不觉中，我就**一头扎进去了**，开始了解关于他的一切。

I found out that he has had **an incredible career**. He stayed active in skateboarding for over 40 years, which is extremely rare in such a **physically demanding sport**.
我发现他拥有一个**非常了不起的职业生涯**。他在滑板领域活跃了超过40年，这在这样一个**对体力要求很高的运动**中是非常少见的。

Not only that, but he was also **involved in developing the video game series** that made him famous worldwide.
不仅如此，他还**参与开发了那款让他全球出名的游戏系列**。

On top of that, he successfully **built a personal brand** and **turned his passion into a multi-million-dollar business**.
除此之外，他还成功**打造了个人品牌**，并且**把自己的兴趣变成了一个数百万美元规模的事业**。

That’s why I really admire him. It’s not just his talent, but his **vision and ability to think beyond the sport**. He didn’t just **succeed in his field**—he **redefined what success looks like**.
这也是为什么我非常钦佩他。不仅仅是因为他的天赋，更是因为他**超越运动本身的眼光和思维能力**。他不仅在自己的领域取得了成功，更是**重新定义了成功的意义**。
