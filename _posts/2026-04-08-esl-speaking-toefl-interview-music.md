## **Q1. How important is music in your life and why?**

### **答题思路（中文）**

这道题最好按照 **“重要性 → 具体作用 → 现实限制 → 总结”** 的顺序来答。
先直接说明音乐在你生活中重不重要。
然后用两个具体功能来支撑，比如它可以帮助你调节情绪，也可以帮助你学习时更专注。
接着补充一个真实的小限制，比如现在没那么多时间听音乐，这会让答案更自然、更像真实口语。
最后再收回来，强调音乐总体上还是很重要的。

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Well, I’d say music is quite important in my life, especially when I’m **in a certain mood**. Listening to music can really **lift my spirits**, **give me confidence**, and **help me stay positive**.

It also helps me **relax and focus**, especially when I’m studying or trying to **keep up with** different subjects. That said, I don’t have as much time to listen to music these days because I don’t always have access to my phone, which is my **main way of listening**. But overall, I still think music **plays an important role** in my life.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **in a certain mood** — 处于某种情绪中
* **lift my spirits** — 让我振作起来
* **give me confidence** — 给我信心
* **help me stay positive** — 帮助我保持积极
* **relax and focus** — 放松并集中注意力
* **keep up with** — 跟上
* **main way of listening** — 主要的听歌方式
* **plays an important role** — 发挥重要作用

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“I’d say music **plays a pretty big role** in my life. Whenever I’m **feeling down or stressed**, listening to music really helps me **unwind** and **puts me in a better mood**.

It also helps me **stay focused** when I’m studying—it kind of **blocks out distractions** and **keeps me on track**. That said, I don’t get to listen to it as much as I’d like these days since I mainly **rely on** my phone. But even so, music is still something I really **value** in my daily life.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **plays a pretty big role** — 发挥很大的作用
* **feeling down or stressed** — 感到低落或有压力
* **unwind** — 放松下来
* **puts me in a better mood** — 让我心情变好
* **stay focused** — 保持专注
* **block out distractions** — 排除干扰
* **keep me on track** — 让我保持在正确状态上
* **rely on** — 依赖
* **value** — 重视

## **Q2. If you had to choose, would you rather create music yourself or listen to music created by others, and why?**

### **答题思路（中文）**

这题可以按照 **“选择 → 原因1（表达情感）→ 原因2（受到启发）→ 总结”** 来组织。
先明确说你更想自己创作音乐，而不是只听别人创作的。
然后解释原因，重点可以放在“音乐是一种表达自我的方式”，有些情绪是语言说不出来的。
接着补充，听别人的音乐也会带来灵感，促使你想创作属于自己的东西。
最后再总结，说你想通过音乐表达自己，这样逻辑会很完整。

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Well, if I had the ability, I’d definitely choose to **create music myself**. I think it’s a really **unique way to express your feelings**, especially in a way that words sometimes can’t.

I also feel **inspired** when I listen to other people’s music—it makes me want to **create something of my own**. So for me, it’s not just about listening, but also about **expressing myself through music**. That’s why I’d love to **give it a try**.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **create music myself** — 自己创作音乐
* **unique way to express your feelings** — 表达情感的独特方式
* **inspired** — 受到启发的
* **create something of my own** — 创作属于我自己的东西
* **expressing myself through music** — 通过音乐表达自己
* **give it a try** — 试一试

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“I’d probably choose to **create music myself**, if I had the skills. I think it’s such a **powerful way to express emotions**, especially things that are hard to **put into words**.

Listening to other people’s music often **sparks ideas** and makes me want to **come up with something of my own**. It’s like **turning inspiration into something personal**. So yeah, I’d love to create music as a way to **express myself** and **share my own perspective**.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **create music myself** — 自己创作音乐
* **powerful way to express emotions** — 很有力量的情感表达方式
* **put into words** — 用语言表达出来
* **spark ideas** — 激发想法
* **come up with something of my own** — 想出属于自己的东西
* **turning inspiration into something personal** — 把灵感变成很个人化的东西
* **express myself** — 表达自己
* **share my own perspective** — 分享我自己的看法/视角

## **Q3. Do you think music should be a required part of children’s education? Why or why not?**

### **答题思路（中文）**

这题适合用 **“应该纳入教育 → 为什么有价值 → 怎么参与 → 未来影响”** 的结构。
先明确表态，说音乐应该成为儿童教育的一部分。
然后解释它的价值，比如孩子可以接触不同领域、发展创造力。
接着不要只说“听音乐”，而要强调参与式活动，比如唱歌、合唱、学乐器。
最后再往未来延伸，说这种经历可能会激发兴趣，甚至影响他们以后的人生方向。

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yeah, I definitely think music should be part of children’s education. It gives kids a chance to **explore different areas**, and music is something they might not get much **exposure to** otherwise.

Also, music education isn’t just about listening—it’s about **taking part in** activities like singing or playing instruments. Through that **hands-on experience**, kids can really feel the power of music and enjoy it more. And who knows, it might even **spark an interest** that they want to **pursue in the future**.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **explore different areas** — 探索不同领域
* **get exposure to** — 接触到
* **take part in** — 参加
* **hands-on experience** — 亲身实践的体验
* **spark an interest** — 激发兴趣
* **pursue in the future** — 将来去追求

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“For sure. I think music should be a **regular part** of children’s education. It **opens the door for** kids to **explore their creativity** and try something different.

And it’s not just about listening to songs—it’s about **getting involved**, like joining a choir or learning an instrument. That kind of **hands-on experience** really **brings music to life**. It can even **plant a seed** and inspire some kids to **take it further in the future**. So yeah, I think it’s definitely **worth including**.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **regular part** — 固定的一部分
* **open the door for** — 为……打开大门
* **explore their creativity** — 探索他们的创造力
* **get involved** — 参与其中
* **hands-on experience** — 亲身实践体验
* **bring music to life** — 让音乐变得鲜活起来
* **plant a seed** — 埋下一颗种子
* **take it further in the future** — 将来进一步发展它
* **worth including** — 值得纳入

## **Q4. Some people believe that live music has a stronger impact than recorded music. Do you agree or disagree with that? Why or why not?**

### **答题思路（中文）**

这题可以用 **“表态同意 → 氛围更强 → 体验更完整 → 感官参与更多 → 总结”** 来答。
先明确表示你同意现场音乐影响力更强。
然后先讲一个最直接的原因：现场的氛围、观众、能量感，是录音很难复制的。
接着再提高一点，说现场音乐不只是“听”，而是一种完整的体验，因为你能看到表演者、感受现场、融入当下。
最后总结成一句：它调动的感官更多，所以影响更强。

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yeah, I totally agree. I think live music has a much stronger impact because of the **atmosphere**. When people come together and enjoy the same performance, it **creates a kind of energy** that recorded music just can’t match.

Also, live music is more than just listening—it’s **an experience**. You get to see the performers, **feel the vibe**, and really **be part of the moment**. So it **engages more of your senses**, not just your hearing. That’s why I think live music is more powerful.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **atmosphere** — 氛围
* **create a kind of energy** — 创造出一种能量感
* **an experience** — 一种体验
* **feel the vibe** — 感受现场氛围
* **be part of the moment** — 成为当下的一部分
* **engage more of your senses** — 调动你更多的感官

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“For sure, I agree with that. Live music just **hits differently**. The atmosphere, the crowd, and the energy all come together to **create something special** that you can’t get from recorded music.

It’s not just about hearing the music—it’s about the **whole experience**. You get to see your favorite artists perform live, feel the energy of the crowd, and really **soak it all in**. It’s much more **immersive** and **memorable**. So yeah, I’d definitely say live music has a stronger impact.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **hit differently** — 感受特别不一样
* **create something special** — 创造出特别的东西
* **the whole experience** — 整体体验
* **soak it all in** — 全身心感受这一切
* **immersive** — 很有沉浸感的
* **memorable** — 难忘的
* **stronger impact** — 更强的影响力

# **High-Impact Vocabulary Summary（这套题最值得背）**

## **1. lift my spirits**

* 中文：让我振作起来
* 用法：适合回答音乐、电影、朋友、运动对情绪的积极影响

## **2. unwind**

* 中文：放松下来
* 用法：比 relax 更自然口语

## **3. block out distractions**

* 中文：排除干扰
* 用法：学习、工作、专注类题目很好用

## **4. keep me on track**

* 中文：让我保持在正确状态上
* 用法：表示帮助你保持专注、持续努力

## **5. put into words**

* 中文：用语言表达出来
* 用法：形容有些感受很难说清楚

## **6. spark ideas**

* 中文：激发想法
* 用法：适合创作、学习、艺术、科技类问题

## **7. hands-on experience**

* 中文：亲身实践体验
* 用法：教育类题目高频好词

## **8. open the door for**

* 中文：为……打开大门
* 用法：特别适合讲教育、机会、成长

## **9. plant a seed**

* 中文：埋下种子
* 用法：表示小时候的经历会对未来产生长期影响

## **10. hit differently**

* 中文：感觉特别不一样
* 用法：很口语，很适合描述现场音乐、电影、回忆、现场比赛
