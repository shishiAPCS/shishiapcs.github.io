<h3>Part 2 &amp; Part 3</h3>
<h4>人物类</h4>
<details class="topic-card" id="sec-1-c-1-t-1"><summary>1. 自学的朋友 <span class="topic-meta">（1.3）</span></summary>
<div class="topic-block"><div class="topic-subheading">Part 2</div>
<p>Describe one of your friends who learned something without a teacher</p>
<p><strong>You should say:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Who he/she is</li>
<li>What he/she learned</li>
<li>Why he/she learned this</li>
<li>And explain whether it would be easier to learn from a teacher</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="topic-block"><div class="topic-subheading">Part 3</div>
<ul>
<li>Is it necessary to keep learning after graduating from school?</li>
<li>Should teachers make learning in their classes fun?</li>
<li>Do you think there are too many subjects for students to learn?</li>
<li>Is it better to focus on a few subjects or to learn many subjects?</li>
<li>Do you think enterprises should provide training for their employees?</li>
<li>Do you think it is good for older adults to continue learning?</li>
</ul>
</div>
</details>
<details class="topic-card" id="sec-1-c-1-t-2"><summary>2. 乐于助人的人 <span class="topic-meta">（1.11）</span></summary>
<div class="topic-block"><div class="topic-subheading">Part 2</div>
<p>Describe a person who often helps others</p>
<p><strong>You should say:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Who this person is</li>
<li>How often he/she helps others</li>
<li>How/why he/she helps others</li>
<li>And how you feel about this person</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="topic-block"><div class="topic-subheading">Part 3</div>
<ul>
<li>Should children help their parents with household chores?</li>
<li>What kind of help do people need when looking for a new job?</li>
<li>Who should people ask for help, colleagues or family members?</li>
<li>Why do you think some people are more willing to help others than some?</li>
<li>Do you think it is important for neighbors to help each other? Why?</li>
<li>How can helping others benefit the person who offers the help?</li>
</ul>
