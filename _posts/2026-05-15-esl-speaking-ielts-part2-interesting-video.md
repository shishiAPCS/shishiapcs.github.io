# A Skateboarding Video That Inspired Me

# 一个让我很受启发的滑板视频

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
I watched a Tony Hawk skateboarding video on YouTube and was inspired by his skill, energy, and lifelong attitude toward practice.
我在 YouTube 上看了一个 Tony Hawk 的滑板视频，被他的技术、状态和终身练习的态度深深打动。

**Best for these topics:**

* an interesting video you watched
* a video that impressed you
* a performance you watched
* a person who inspired you
* something you saw online
* a lesson you learned from a video
* a sport you are interested in

**Emotion path:**
casual → curious → impressed → amazed → inspired → reflective

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Video:** a skateboarding performance video
（视频：一个滑板表演视频）

**Person:** Tony Hawk
（人物：Tony Hawk）

**How I found it:** YouTube algorithm pushed it into my feed
（如何看到：YouTube 算法推荐给我）

**Why it impressed me:** he was still performing difficult tricks in his 50s
（印象深刻的原因：他五十多岁仍然能完成高难度动作）

**Sport difficulty:** skateboarding is physically demanding
（运动难度：滑板对体力和身体要求很高）

**Key message:** keep improving and keep practicing
（核心信息：不断进步，不断练习）

**Takeaway:** lifelong learning and persistence
（收获：终身学习和坚持）

## C. Must-know chunks

## 必备表达

* **stand out to me (idiomatic)**
  给我留下深刻印象 / 很突出

* **come across something (idiomatic)**
  偶然看到 / 无意中刷到

* **in the first place (idiomatic)**
  一开始 / 原本

* **push it into my feed**
  把它推荐到我的信息流里

* **end up doing something (idiomatic)**
  最后做了某事 / 结果做了某事

* **turn out to be… (idiomatic)**
  结果是……

* **leave a deep impression on me**
  给我留下很深的印象

* **land tricks**
  成功完成滑板动作

* **perform at a high level**
  保持很高水平的表现

* **physically demanding**
  对身体要求很高的

* **retire way before that**
  早在那之前就退役

* **still out there (idiomatic)**
  仍然活跃在那个领域 / 还在坚持

* **push oneself (idiomatic)**
  挑战自己 / 逼自己继续进步

* **personal mantra**
  个人信念 / 人生格言

* **for as long as he can**
  只要他还能做到

* **biggest takeaway (idiomatic)**
  最大的收获 / 最重要的启发

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

One video that really **stood out to me** was a skateboarding performance video I watched on YouTube. Basically, I **came across it** on YouTube, and it was a skateboarding performance by Tony Hawk.
有一个让我印象特别深的视频，是我在 YouTube 上看到的一个滑板表演视频。简单来说，我是在 YouTube 上偶然刷到的，视频里是 Tony Hawk 的滑板表演。

I watched it last week, and honestly, I wasn’t even trying to find that video **in the first place**—the algorithm just **pushed it into my feed**. I got a notification, clicked on it, and **ended up watching the whole thing**. So it wasn’t something I was specifically looking for.
我是上周看的这个视频。说实话，我一开始根本不是专门去找这个视频的，是平台算法直接把它推荐到了我的信息流里。我收到一个通知，就点进去看了，结果最后把整个视频都看完了。所以它并不是我特意搜索出来的内容。

But anyway, it **turned out to be** a great video, and it **left a really deep impression on me** because seeing Tony Hawk still **landing tricks** and performing at such a high level in his 50s was honestly incredible.
但不管怎么说，它最后证明是一个非常棒的视频，也给我留下了很深的印象。因为看到 Tony Hawk 在五十多岁的时候，仍然能完成滑板动作，并且保持这么高的水平，真的很不可思议。

In a sport that’s this **physically demanding**, a lot of skateboarders would’ve retired way before that—maybe in their 30s or 40s. But he’s **still out there**, still **pushing himself**, and that really amazed me and inspired me.
滑板是一项对身体要求很高的运动，很多滑板运动员可能早在这个年纪之前就退役了，也许三四十岁就不再比赛或表演了。但他仍然活跃在这个领域，仍然不断挑战自己，这一点真的让我很惊讶，也很受鼓舞。

At the end of the performance, he also shared his mindset—kind of his **personal mantra**—about always improving and always practicing **for as long as he can**.
在表演的最后，他还分享了自己的心态，可以说是他的一个人生信念：只要自己还能做到，就要一直进步、一直练习。

I think that’s the kind of attitude all of us should have: keep learning, keep getting better, and never stop practicing. That was probably my **biggest takeaway** from the video, and that’s why I found it so interesting.
我觉得这正是我们每个人都应该有的态度：不断学习，不断变好，不要停止练习。这大概就是我从这个视频里得到的最大启发，也是为什么我觉得它这么有意思。
