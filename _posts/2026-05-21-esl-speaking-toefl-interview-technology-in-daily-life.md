## **Q1. How important is technology in your day-to-day activities?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“直接回答 → 两个例子 → 影响 → 总结”** 来回答。
先直接说明科技在日常生活中很重要。
然后给两个具体例子，比如手机支付、开门、联系他人。
接着说明科技带来的影响，比如更方便、更高效、更容易保持联系。
最后总结：科技已经成为日常生活中不可缺少的一部分。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Well, I have to say technology plays a huge role in my daily life. First, it makes my life much easier. I don’t even need to carry cash with me all the time. I can use my phone to pay bills through Alipay or WeChat Pay, and I also use it to unlock the entrance to my apartment building. So basically, it’s like my virtual key. Besides that, technology also helps me connect with people, especially those who live far away. I can make video calls or send messages, and I can do it all on my phone. So that means we can stay connected, and whenever we want to, we can reach out to each other.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **plays a huge role** — 起着非常重要的作用
* **carry cash** — 带现金
* **pay bills through...** — 通过……支付账单
* **unlock the entrance** — 解锁入口 / 开门
* **virtual key** — 虚拟钥匙
* **connect with people** — 与人联系
* **stay connected** — 保持联系
* **reach out to each other** — 联系彼此

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“Honestly, technology is a huge part of my everyday life. It makes things so much easier. I don’t have to carry cash around anymore because I can pay bills or buy things with apps like Alipay and WeChat Pay. I also use my phone to get into my apartment building, so in a way, it’s basically my digital key. On top of that, technology helps me stay in touch with people who live far away. I can video call them, text them, or reach out anytime, all from my phone. So overall, technology makes my life more convenient and helps me feel connected.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **a huge part of my everyday life** — 日常生活中很重要的一部分
* **carry cash around** — 随身带现金
* **buy things with apps** — 用应用程序买东西
* **digital key** — 电子钥匙 / 数字钥匙
* **on top of that** — 除此之外
* **stay in touch with...** — 和……保持联系
* **video call** — 视频通话
* **text someone** — 给某人发消息
* **feel connected** — 感到和别人有联系

## **Q2. Have you ever felt frustrated while using technology? What happened?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“直接回答 → 一个具体事件 → 出了什么问题 → 感受和结果 → 总结”** 来回答。
这题问的是 **What happened?**，所以最好讲一个具体的过去经历。
不要讲太多普通情况，比如“有时候网不好”，那样不够具体。
一个清楚的例子比两个匆忙的例子更好。
注意主要使用过去时，尤其是 **I was recording / I realized / I had spent / I ended up with...**

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yeah, definitely. It happens from time to time. There was one time when I was recording a tutorial for my students, and after I finished, I noticed my microphone hadn’t been working properly. I had already spent hours on it, and I ended up with a video with no sound. That was when I felt really frustrated. I literally almost smashed my keyboard, but I calmed myself down and pulled myself together. I told myself, ‘Well, I can do it again. I still have time.’ So that was the last time I dealt with a technology failure.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **from time to time** — 时不时
* **record a tutorial** — 录制教程
* **my microphone hadn’t been working properly** — 我的麦克风一直没有正常工作
* **had already spent hours on it** — 已经花了好几个小时在这上面
* **ended up with...** — 最后得到 / 结果是……
* **with no sound** — 没有声音
* **felt really frustrated** — 感到非常沮丧
* **calmed myself down** — 让自己冷静下来
* **pulled myself together** — 让自己振作起来
* **technology failure** — 技术故障

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“Yeah, definitely. One time, I was recording a tutorial for my students, and after I finished, I realized my mic hadn’t been working the whole time. I had spent hours on it, but the final video had no sound at all. I was so frustrated that I almost slammed my keyboard, but I took a deep breath and pulled myself together. I reminded myself that I still had time to redo it. So yeah, that was probably one of the most frustrating tech problems I’ve dealt with.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **one time** — 有一次
* **my mic hadn’t been working the whole time** — 我的麦克风整个过程都没有工作
* **the final video** — 最终的视频
* **had no sound at all** — 完全没有声音
* **almost slammed my keyboard** — 差点猛拍键盘
* **took a deep breath** — 深呼吸
* **redo it** — 重新做一遍
* **tech problem** — 技术问题
* **one of the most frustrating...** — 最令人沮丧的……之一
* **I’ve dealt with** — 我处理过的 / 我遇到过的

## **Q3. Some people believe technology will replace many jobs, while others think it will create more opportunities. What is your opinion and why?**

### **答题思路**

这道题适合用 **“平衡观点 → 一方面 → 另一方面 → 总结”**。
因为题目本身给了两个观点，所以学生可以采取一个比较安全的中间立场。
不要两边都讲得太散，要保持清楚结构。
可以说：**科技会取代一些重复性工作，但也会创造新的机会。**
最后总结：关键不是害怕科技，而是学习新技能、适应变化。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Well, I agree that technology will replace a lot of jobs, but it will also help create more opportunities. For example, many repetitive jobs, like data entry, may definitely be replaced with the development of AI and more advanced tools. On the other hand, jobs that require creativity or human interaction will remain important because these are things AI technology is not naturally good at. For example, if you need a tutor to help you with math, you probably want to talk to a real person instead of a chatbot. So I guess creative jobs and jobs with more human interaction will still be needed.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **replace a lot of jobs** — 取代很多工作
* **create more opportunities** — 创造更多机会
* **repetitive jobs** — 重复性工作
* **data entry** — 数据录入
* **with the development of AI** — 随着人工智能的发展
* **advanced tools** — 更先进的工具
* **on the other hand** — 另一方面
* **require creativity** — 需要创造力
* **human interaction** — 人与人的互动
* **remain important** — 仍然重要
* **still be needed** — 仍然被需要

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“I think technology will replace some jobs, but it will also create new opportunities. For example, repetitive jobs like data entry may disappear as AI and automation become more advanced. But jobs that require creativity, emotional support, or real human interaction will still be valuable. For instance, if you need help with math, you might prefer a real tutor over a chatbot because a person can understand your struggles better. So overall, I think technology will change the job market, but it won’t replace everything.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **create new opportunities** — 创造新的机会
* **may disappear** — 可能会消失
* **AI and automation** — 人工智能和自动化
* **become more advanced** — 变得更先进
* **emotional support** — 情感支持
* **real human interaction** — 真实的人际互动
* **still be valuable** — 仍然有价值
* **prefer A over B** — 比起 B 更喜欢 A
* **understand your struggles** — 理解你的困难
* **change the job market** — 改变就业市场
* **won’t replace everything** — 不会取代一切

## **Q4. Some people believe that reliance on technology is reducing our ability to think independently and solve problems. Do you agree or disagree with this belief? Why or why not?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“明确立场 → 原因 → 具体例子 → 结果 → 总结”** 来回答。
这题不一定要两边都讲，选择一边会更容易。
可以说：**我基本同意，因为过度依赖科技会削弱独立思考和解决问题的能力。**
例子可以用学生使用 AI 写作业：他们直接复制答案，而不是自己思考。
最后补一句平衡观点：科技本身不是坏事，但过度依赖会有害。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Well, I agree that reliance on technology is reducing our ability to think critically, and as a result, our problem-solving ability is also being reduced. For example, nowadays, a lot of students use AI chatbots to help them with their homework. But what many students do is ask AI questions, or even upload a picture of the questions, hoping to get the answers directly. They don’t really think deeply; they just copy the answers. So in a way, this process reduces their ability to think critically and weakens their learning process. That’s why I think technology can have a negative influence.”

#### **Version 1 重点词汇**

* **reliance on technology** — 对科技的依赖
* **think critically** — 批判性思考
* **as a result** — 因此
* **problem-solving ability** — 解决问题的能力
* **AI chatbots** — AI 聊天机器人
* **upload a picture of the questions** — 上传题目的照片
* **get the answers directly** — 直接得到答案
* **think deeply** — 深入思考
* **copy the answers** — 抄答案
* **weaken their learning process** — 削弱他们的学习过程
* **have a negative influence** — 产生负面影响

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“I mostly agree that relying too much on technology can weaken our critical thinking and problem-solving skills. For example, many students now use AI chatbots for homework. Instead of working through the questions themselves, they simply type in the problem or upload a photo and wait for the answer. Then they copy it without really understanding the logic behind it. Over time, this can make students less willing to think independently or solve problems on their own. So overall, I think technology is useful, but depending on it too much can hurt the way we learn.”

#### **Version 2 重点词汇**

* **I mostly agree that...** — 我基本同意……
* **relying too much on technology** — 过度依赖科技
* **weaken our critical thinking** — 削弱我们的批判性思维
* **problem-solving skills** — 解决问题的技能
* **work through the questions** — 自己一步步做题 / 思考题目
* **type in the problem** — 输入问题
* **wait for the answer** — 等答案
* **the logic behind it** — 背后的逻辑
* **over time** — 久而久之
* **less willing to...** — 更不愿意……
* **think independently** — 独立思考
* **solve problems on their own** — 自己解决问题
* **hurt the way we learn** — 损害我们的学习方式
