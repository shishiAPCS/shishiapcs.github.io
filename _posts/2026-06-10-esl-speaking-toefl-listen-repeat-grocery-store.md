# TOEFL Speaking Listen and Repeat Practice

## Topic: Working at a Grocery Store

**Goal:** Hear the sentence, remember the chunks, and repeat the full sentence accurately.

---

# 1. Sentence Chunks

## 1. Milk and yogurt are in the dairy section.

**Chunks:**
Milk and yogurt / are in the dairy section.

**Memory note:**
记住结构：**items + location**
注意 **dairy section = 乳制品区**，不要听成 **diary**。

---

## 2. Bread is freshly baked on site every day.

**Chunks:**
Bread / is freshly baked on site / every day.

**Memory note:**
核心信息是：**freshly baked on site**
结尾 **every day** 不要漏掉。

---

## 3. We have a huge selection of fresh fruits and vegetables.

**Chunks:**
We have / a huge selection of / fresh fruits and vegetables.

**Memory note:**
重点词块：**a huge selection of = 很多种……**
后面接具体商品。

---

## 4. This flyer has all our weekly deals and special discounts.

**Chunks:**
This flyer / has all our weekly deals / and special discounts.

**Memory note:**
后面有两个优惠信息：
**weekly deals + special discounts**

---

## 5. The checkout counter is located at the front of the store.

**Chunks:**
The checkout counter / is located / at the front of the store.

**Memory note:**
**checkout counter = 收银台**
**at the front of the store = 在店内靠前的位置 / 在商店前部**

---

## 6. If you need help while learning, please don’t hesitate to ask a full-time store associate.

**Chunks:**
If you need help while learning, / please don’t hesitate to ask / a full-time store associate.

**Memory note:**
这里 **while learning** 指：
**while learning the job / getting familiar with the work**
也就是“在学习这份兼职工作 / 熟悉工作流程时”。

核心主干：
**If you need help, please ask a store associate.**

---

## 7. Download our app and sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about upcoming sales.

**Chunks:**
Download our app / and sign up for our newsletter / to stay informed / about upcoming sales.

**Memory note:**
两个动作：**download + sign up for**
一个目的：**to stay informed about upcoming sales**

# 3. Pronunciation Traps / Confusing Words

| Pair / Word              | Meaning                                  | Listening Tip                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **dairy / diary**        | dairy = 乳制品；diary = 日记                   | **dairy** sounds like “DAIR-ee”; **diary** has an extra sound: “DIE-uh-ree”                                                  |
| **section / selection**  | section = 区域；selection = 选择、种类           | **selection** is longer: se-LEC-tion                                                                                         |
| **on site / online**     | on site = 在现场；online = 在线                | **on site** has two words                                                                                                    |
| **site / sight / cite**  | site = 地点；sight = 视力/景象；cite = 引用        | Same sound, different spelling and meaning                                                                                   |
| **weekly / weakly**      | weekly = 每周；weakly = 虚弱地                 | Same sound; meaning depends on context                                                                                       |
| **sales / sells**        | sales = 促销/销售；sells = 销售这个动作             | Same sound; **sales** is a noun                                                                                              |
| **checkout / check out** | checkout counter = 收银台；check out = 查看/结账 | **checkout counter** uses **checkout** as part of a noun phrase.                                                             |
| **associate**            | noun: 店员；同事                              | In **store associate**, it is a noun. It sounds like “uh-SOH-shee-it.” The verb **associate** sounds like “uh-SOH-shee-ate.” |

# 4. Key Sentence Patterns

## Pattern 1

**A and B are in the + place.**

From the sentence:
Milk and yogurt are in the dairy section.

More examples:

1. Apples and oranges are in the fruit section.
2. Pens and notebooks are in the school supply section.

---

## Pattern 2

**Something is freshly made/baked/prepared + place/time.**

From the sentence:
Bread is freshly baked on site every day.

More examples:

1. Coffee is freshly made every morning.
2. Pizza is freshly baked in the kitchen.

---

## Pattern 3

**We have a huge selection of + nouns.**

From the sentence:
We have a huge selection of fresh fruits and vegetables.

More examples:

1. We have a huge selection of drinks.
2. We have a huge selection of snacks.

---

## Pattern 4

**If you need help while + verb-ing, please don’t hesitate to ask + person.**

From the sentence:
If you need help while learning, please don’t hesitate to ask a full-time store associate.

More examples:

1. If you need help while studying, please don’t hesitate to ask your teacher.
2. If you need help while practicing, please don’t hesitate to ask a classmate.

# 6. Student Reminder

For Listen and Repeat, don’t memorize word by word.

Use this method:

1. **Catch the chunks.**
2. **Remember the key words.**
3. **Say the sentence in meaning groups.**
4. **Don’t drop the ending.**
5. **Watch out for confusing words.**
