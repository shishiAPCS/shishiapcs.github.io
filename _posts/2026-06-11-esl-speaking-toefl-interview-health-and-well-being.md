## **Q1. Do you have any specific routines or practices you use to maintain your physical well-being? If so, what are they?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“直接回答 → 一个运动习惯 → 一个生活习惯 → 总结”** 来回答。  
先直接回答 **Yes, I do.**  
然后讲一个具体的 routine，比如 **jogging**，并说明频率和时间。  
接着补充一个简单但重要的习惯，比如 **washing my hands regularly**。  
最后总结：保持健康不只是运动，也包括每天保护身体的小习惯。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yes, I do. One routine I try to keep is jogging. I pretty much jog every day—sometimes after school, and on weekends, usually in the morning. It helps me get my body moving, clear my head, and stay in decent shape.

Another small but important habit is washing my hands regularly. It may sound simple, but I think it really matters. Our hands pick up germs all the time, so if we touch our food without washing them, we might get sick. So for me, staying healthy is not only about exercise. It’s also about building small daily habits that protect my body.”

#### **Version 1 核心词汇**

* **routine** — 日常习惯
* **jogging** — 慢跑
* **pretty much every day** — 基本上每天
* **on weekends** — 在周末
* **get my body moving** — 让身体动起来
* **clear my head** — 放松头脑 / 清醒头脑
* **stay in decent shape** — 保持还不错的身体状态
* **regularly** — 经常地 / 有规律地
* **pick up germs** — 沾上细菌
* **protect my body** — 保护身体

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“Yeah, I try to stay healthy by keeping a few simple daily habits. The main one is jogging. I usually jog after school, and on weekends, I often do it in the morning. It helps me stay active, clear my mind, and feel more energetic.

Besides exercise, I also pay attention to small hygiene habits, like washing my hands regularly. It sounds basic, but it really makes a difference because our hands come into contact with germs all the time. So to me, staying healthy is not just about working out. It’s also about doing small things every day to take care of my body.”

#### **Version 2 核心词汇**

* **daily habits** — 日常习惯
* **stay active** — 保持活跃
* **clear my mind** — 放松头脑
* **feel more energetic** — 感觉更有精力
* **besides exercise** — 除了运动以外
* **hygiene habits** — 卫生习惯
* **make a difference** — 起作用 / 有影响
* **come into contact with germs** — 接触到细菌
* **working out** — 锻炼
* **take care of my body** — 照顾好身体

## **Q2. Can you describe any dietary choices or habits that you follow to stay healthy? Give details to explain your answer.**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“直接回答 → 食物选择 → 控制食量 → 总结”** 来回答。  
先说明自己有一些饮食习惯。  
第一个重点讲 **choose foods that are lower in oil and sugar**，并用水煮蛋和煎蛋做对比。  
第二个重点讲 **eating in moderation**，也就是不要吃太多、不要吃撑。  
最后总结：健康饮食不只是选择正确的食物，也要控制分量，知道什么时候停下来。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yes, I do have a few dietary habits that help me stay healthy. First, I try to choose foods that are lower in oil and sugar. For example, when I have eggs, I usually prefer hard-boiled eggs over fried eggs, like sunny-side-up eggs, because they are lighter and contain less oil.

Another habit I follow is eating in moderation. I try not to overeat or stuff myself, even when the food tastes good. If I eat too much, I usually feel heavy and tired afterward, and it puts extra pressure on my digestive system. So I think a healthy diet is not just about choosing the right food. It’s also about controlling portions and knowing when to stop.”

#### **Version 1 核心词汇**

* **dietary habits** — 饮食习惯
* **lower in oil and sugar** — 油和糖更少
* **hard-boiled eggs** — 水煮蛋
* **fried eggs** — 煎蛋
* **sunny-side-up eggs** — 单面煎蛋
* **lighter** — 更清淡的
* **eating in moderation** — 适量饮食
* **overeat** — 吃太多
* **digestive system** — 消化系统
* **controlling portions** — 控制分量

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“Yes, I try to be careful with what I eat. One habit is choosing lighter foods whenever possible. For example, if I’m having eggs, I would usually go for hard-boiled eggs instead of fried eggs because they contain less oil and feel healthier.

I also try to control my portions. Even if the food is really good, I remind myself not to eat until I’m completely full. Otherwise, I may feel sleepy, heavy, and uncomfortable afterward. So overall, I think eating healthily is about two things: making better food choices and knowing when to stop.”

#### **Version 2 核心词汇**

* **be careful with what I eat** — 注意自己的饮食
* **lighter foods** — 更清淡的食物
* **whenever possible** — 只要可以的话
* **go for...** — 选择……
* **contain less oil** — 含油更少
* **control my portions** — 控制食量
* **completely full** — 完全吃饱 / 吃撑
* **uncomfortable afterward** — 之后不舒服
* **make better food choices** — 做出更好的食物选择
* **know when to stop** — 知道什么时候停下来

## **Q3. If you could make one significant change to your diet or exercise routine to stay healthy, what would it be? Why would you make that particular choice?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“选择一个改变 → 解释不是极端做法 → 给出原因 → 说明结果”** 来回答。  
这里可以说：**eat fewer refined carbs and add more protein**。  
注意不要把碳水说得太绝对，比如不要直接说 **carbs cause heart disease**。  
更稳妥的说法是：不是完全不吃碳水，而是减少精制碳水，比如米饭、面条、面包和甜食。  
然后说明蛋白质的好处：帮助维持肌肉、增加饱腹感、让能量更稳定。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“If I could make one significant change, I would adjust my diet by eating fewer refined carbs and adding more protein. I don’t mean cutting out carbs completely, because our bodies still need them. But I think I rely too much on things like rice, noodles, bread, and sweet snacks.

I would make this change because protein helps build and maintain muscle, and it also keeps me full for a longer time. That means I would probably snack less and have more stable energy throughout the day. For me, this would be a realistic way to stay healthier, lose some fat, and build a leaner body.”

#### **Version 1 核心词汇**

* **significant change** — 重要改变
* **adjust my diet** — 调整饮食
* **refined carbs** — 精制碳水
* **add more protein** — 增加蛋白质
* **cut out carbs completely** — 完全不吃碳水
* **rely too much on...** — 过度依赖……
* **build and maintain muscle** — 增长并维持肌肉
* **keep me full** — 让我有饱腹感
* **stable energy** — 稳定的能量
* **a leaner body** — 更精瘦的身体

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“If I could change one thing, I’d probably change the way I eat by cutting down on refined carbs and adding more protein to my meals. I’m not saying I would stop eating carbs completely, because that wouldn’t be realistic or healthy. But I do think I eat too much rice, noodles, bread, and sweet snacks.

The reason I’d make this change is that protein can help me build muscle and stay full longer. If I feel full for longer, I won’t snack as much, and my energy level may become more stable during the day. So for me, this would be a practical change because it could help me stay fit, lose some fat, and build a healthier body.”

#### **Version 2 核心词汇**

* **cut down on...** — 减少……
* **add more protein to my meals** — 每餐增加蛋白质
* **I’m not saying...** — 我不是说……
* **realistic** — 现实的 / 可行的
* **stay full longer** — 更长时间有饱腹感
* **snack as much** — 吃那么多零食
* **energy level** — 能量水平
* **a practical change** — 一个实际可行的改变
* **stay fit** — 保持健康 / 保持身材
* **build a healthier body** — 打造更健康的身体

## **Q4. Some people believe that mental health is just as important as physical health and should be given equal attention. Do you agree or disagree with this viewpoint? Why?**

### **答题思路**

这道题可以按照 **“明确立场 → 两者关系 → 正面例子 → 反面例子 → 总结”** 来回答。  
先直接说自己同意。  
然后说明 **mental health and physical health are closely connected**。  
接着从正面讲：心理状态好时，人更有动力吃好、睡好、运动。  
再从反面讲：压力大、焦虑时，身体健康也会受影响。  
最后总结：健康的身体也需要健康的心理。

---

### **Version 1 (Natural & Smooth)**

“Yes, I agree with this viewpoint. I think mental health is just as important as physical health because the two are closely connected. When people are mentally healthy, they usually have more energy and motivation to take care of themselves. For example, they may eat better, sleep better, and exercise more regularly.

However, when people are stressed, anxious, or under too much pressure, their physical health can also suffer. They may lose their appetite, sleep badly, or stop exercising. Over time, this can make them feel weaker and even make them more likely to get sick. So in my opinion, we should pay equal attention to mental and physical health because a healthy body also needs a healthy mind.”

#### **Version 1 核心词汇**

* **mental health** — 心理健康
* **physical health** — 身体健康
* **just as important as...** — 和……一样重要
* **closely connected** — 紧密相关
* **energy and motivation** — 精力和动力
* **take care of themselves** — 照顾自己
* **under too much pressure** — 承受太大压力
* **lose their appetite** — 食欲下降
* **suffer** — 受影响 / 变差
* **pay equal attention to...** — 对……给予同等重视

---

### **Version 2 (More Expressive & Idiomatic)**

“Yes, I completely agree. Mental health deserves just as much attention as physical health because they affect each other. When someone is in a good mental state, they are more likely to sleep well, eat regularly, exercise, and make healthier choices.

But if someone is constantly stressed or anxious, their body may suffer too. They might lose their appetite, sleep poorly, or feel too tired to exercise. Over time, this can weaken their physical condition. So I believe we shouldn’t separate mental health from physical health. A truly healthy life requires both a healthy body and a healthy mind.”

#### **Version 2 核心词汇**

* **completely agree** — 完全同意
* **deserve attention** — 应该得到关注
* **affect each other** — 互相影响
* **in a good mental state** — 处于良好的心理状态
* **make healthier choices** — 做出更健康的选择
* **constantly stressed** — 一直压力大
* **sleep poorly** — 睡眠不好
* **weaken their physical condition** — 削弱身体状况
* **separate A from B** — 把 A 和 B 分开
* **a truly healthy life** — 真正健康的生活
