# Meeting Tony Hawk in Person

# 想亲眼见到 Tony Hawk

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
I would love to meet Tony Hawk because he was part of my childhood memories and later became someone I truly admired.
我很想见 Tony Hawk，因为他既是我童年记忆的一部分，后来也成为了我真正钦佩的人。

**Best for these topics:**

* a famous person you would like to meet
* a celebrity you admire
* a childhood dream
* a person related to sports
* a person who influenced you
* someone you want to talk to

**Emotion path:**
nostalgic → curious → fascinated → excited → fulfilled

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Who:** Tony Hawk, famous skateboarder
（人物：Tony Hawk，著名滑板运动员）

**First contact:** played his video game in elementary school
（最初接触：小学时玩过他的游戏）

**Misunderstanding:** thought he was just a game character
（小误会：以为他只是游戏人物）

**Rediscovery:** got into skateboarding in high school
（重新认识：高中开始接触滑板）

**Ideal meeting place:** skate park
（理想见面地点：滑板公园）

**Reason:** childhood dream + real admiration
（原因：童年梦想 + 真正钦佩）

## C. Must-know chunks

## 必备表达

* **one celebrity I’ve always wanted to meet**
  一个我一直想见的名人

* **get to know someone’s name**
  知道某人的名字 / 开始认识这个名字

* **at the time**
  当时

* **later on**
  后来

* **get into something (idiomatic)**
  开始喜欢上 / 开始投入某件事

* **fall down a rabbit hole (idiomatic)**
  一头扎进某件事里，越了解越多

* **if I had the chance**
  如果我有机会的话

* **eye-opening experience (idiomatic)**
  开眼界的经历

* **once-in-a-lifetime experience (idiomatic)**
  一生难得一次的经历

* **a dream come true (idiomatic)**
  梦想成真

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

To be honest, there’s one celebrity I’ve always wanted to meet—**Tony Hawk**. He’s one of the most famous and successful skateboarders in the world.
说实话，有一位名人是我一直很想见的，那就是 **Tony Hawk**。他是世界上最有名、最成功的滑板运动员之一。

I first heard about him when I was in elementary school. I used to play this video game series called **Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater**, and that’s how I got to know his name. At the time, I actually thought he was just **a character in the game**.
我第一次听说他是在小学的时候。那时候我经常玩一个叫 **《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》** 的滑板游戏系列，也就是通过这个游戏知道了他的名字。当时我甚至以为他只是**游戏里的一个角色**。

Later on, in high school, I **got into skateboarding myself**. I started watching videos and reading about the sport, and that’s when I discovered him again. Before I knew it, I had **fallen down a rabbit hole** learning everything about him—his tricks, his career, and how **influential** he is in skateboarding.
后来到了高中，我自己也开始**喜欢上滑板**。我开始看滑板视频，也会读一些关于这项运动的内容，然后我又一次认识了他。不知不觉中，我就**一头扎进去**，开始了解关于他的一切，包括他的动作、职业生涯，以及他在滑板界有多么**有影响力**。

If I had the chance, I’d love to meet him at a skate park, because I really want to see him **perform his tricks in person**. I think it would be such an **eye-opening** and **once-in-a-lifetime experience**.
如果有机会的话，我很想在滑板公园见到他，因为我真的想**亲眼看他做那些滑板动作**。我觉得那一定会是一次非常**开眼界**、而且**一生难得的经历**。

The main reason I want to meet him is that it’s been **a childhood dream of mine**. When I was younger, I didn’t even realize he was a real person. So being able to meet him and actually talk to him would honestly feel like **a dream come true**.
我想见他的主要原因是，这对我来说一直是**一个童年梦想**。小时候我甚至不知道他是真实存在的人。所以如果能真正见到他，并且和他说上几句话，那真的会感觉像是**梦想成真**。
