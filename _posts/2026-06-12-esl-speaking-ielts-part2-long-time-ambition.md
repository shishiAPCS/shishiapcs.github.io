# 长远志向 (5.13)

# Describe an ambition that you have had for a long time

**You should say:**

* What it is
* What you did for it
* When you can achieve it
* And explain why you have this ambition

---

# My Long-Time Skateboarding Ambition

# 我的长期滑板梦想

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
I have wanted to become a semi-professional skateboarder since childhood, and although it may be difficult now, I still don’t want to give up on this dream.
我从小就想成为一名半职业滑板手，虽然现在实现起来可能很难，但我仍然不想放弃这个梦想。

**Best for these topics:**

* a long-term ambition
* a childhood dream
* a difficult goal
* a sport you want to improve at
* something you have worked hard for
* a personal challenge
* something on your bucket list

**Emotion path:**
nostalgic → passionate → determined → injured → realistic → hopeful

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Ambition:** become a semi-professional skateboarder
（志向：成为一名半职业滑板手）

**How long:** since childhood
（持续时间：从小时候开始）

**First inspiration:** Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater video game
（最初灵感：Tony Hawk 的滑板游戏）

**What I did:** bought a skateboard, watched videos, researched Tony Hawk, practiced daily
（做过的努力：买滑板、看视频、研究 Tony Hawk、每天练习）

**Setback:** hurt my back and took half a year to recover
（挫折：伤到了背，花了半年恢复）

**Current reality:** in my 30s, harder to pursue seriously
（现实情况：现在三十多岁了，认真追求这个目标更难）

**Deeper reason:** still a dream, still on my bucket list
（深层原因：它仍然是梦想，也还在人生愿望清单里）

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

Well, to be honest, one ambition I’ve had for a very long time is to become **a semi-professional skateboarder**. I’ve had this dream since I was a kid, when I first played **Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater**. That’s how I **fell in love with skateboarding**.
说实话，我有了很久的一个志向，就是成为一名**半职业滑板手**。这个梦想从我小时候就开始了，当时我第一次玩 **《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》** 这个游戏，也就是从那时起，我**爱上了滑板**。

After that, I bought a skateboard and started practicing. There was one period of my life when I **seriously considered becoming a professional skateboarder**. So I started watching Tony Hawk’s skateboarding videos. Before I knew it, I had **fallen down a rabbit hole**, researching and learning everything about him.
后来，我买了一块滑板，开始练习。有一段时间，我甚至**认真考虑过成为一名职业滑板手**。所以我开始看 Tony Hawk 的滑板视频。不知不觉中，我就**一头扎进去了**，开始研究并了解关于他的一切。

I didn’t just watch his videos. I also practiced **on a daily basis**, sometimes even too hard. One time, I hurt my back, and it took me **half a year to recover**.
我不只是看他的视频。我也会**每天练习**，有时候甚至练得太猛。有一次，我伤到了背，结果花了**半年时间才恢复**。

As for when I might achieve this goal, I think it’s probably **a little far-fetched**, because I’m in my 30s now, and I’m getting a little old to **pursue skateboarding seriously**.
至于我什么时候能实现这个目标，我觉得它现在可能**有点不太现实**。因为我已经三十多岁了，要认真把滑板作为一个目标去追，确实有点晚了。

But this ambition is still **on my bucket list**, because it is one of my dreams, and I still want to **make it happen**. I know it’s hard to achieve, but I still don’t want to **give up on it**.
但这个志向依然在我的**人生愿望清单**里，因为它一直是我的梦想之一，我还是想努力**让它实现**。我知道这很难做到，但我仍然不想**放弃它**。
