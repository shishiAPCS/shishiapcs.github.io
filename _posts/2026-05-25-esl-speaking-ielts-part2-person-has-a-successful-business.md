# Describe a person you know who has a successful business

**You should say:**

* Who this person is
* How you got to know him/her
* Why and how he/she started the business
* What business he/she does
* And explain why you think the business is successful

---

# Tony Hawk’s Skateboarding Business Empire

# Tony Hawk 的滑板商业帝国

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
Tony Hawk turned his skateboarding talent and reputation into a successful personal brand and business empire.
Tony Hawk 把自己的滑板天赋和名气，发展成了一个成功的个人品牌和商业帝国。

**Best for these topics:**

* a person who has a successful business
* a successful person
* a sportsperson you admire
* a famous person you admire
* someone who turned passion into a career
* a person with a strong personal brand

**Emotion path:**
curious → interested → impressed → admiring → inspired

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Who:** Tony Hawk, a famous skateboarder and businessman
（人物：Tony Hawk，著名滑板运动员和商人）

**How I knew him:** from the Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater video games
（如何认识：通过《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》游戏系列）

**Deeper interest:** got into skateboarding and watched videos on YouTube
（深入了解：开始玩滑板，并在 YouTube 上看相关视频）

**Business type:** skate gear, clothing, video games, brand collaborations
（生意类型：滑板装备、服装、电子游戏、品牌合作）

**Business model:** built business around his name and reputation
（商业模式：围绕自己的名字和声誉打造商业）

**Why successful:** talent + passion + smart branding
（成功原因：天赋 + 热爱 + 聪明的品牌经营）

**Personal reflection:** passion can grow into something much bigger
（个人感受：热爱可以发展成更大的事业）

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

A person I really admire in business is **Tony Hawk**. I first discovered him as a kid from the **Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater** games, but later, when I **got into skateboarding myself**, I started paying a lot more attention to who he actually was.
我在商业方面非常佩服的一个人是 **Tony Hawk**。我小时候是通过 **《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》** 这款游戏系列认识他的。后来，当我自己也开始喜欢上滑板之后，我才开始更加关注他到底是一个什么样的人。

At one point, I got completely **hooked** watching skateboarding videos on YouTube, and before I knew it, I was deep in the **Tony Hawk rabbit hole**, learning about his career and **business ventures**. I found out he didn’t just become famous as a skateboarder — he also built a huge business around his **name and reputation**.
有一段时间，我完全迷上了在 YouTube 上看滑板视频。不知不觉中，我就深深入了 **Tony Hawk 这个“坑”**，开始了解他的职业生涯和各种**商业项目**。我发现他并不只是作为滑板运动员出名，他还围绕自己的**名字和声誉**打造了一个庞大的商业体系。

He **partnered with companies** to make skateboards, wheels, trucks, clothing, and all kinds of **skate gear**. He also **licensed his name** to video games and collaborated with fashion brands on **limited-edition collections**. So in a way, his biggest business asset is really his **personal brand**.
他和不同公司合作，推出滑板、轮子、桥架、服装，以及各种**滑板装备**。他还把自己的名字授权给电子游戏使用，也和时尚品牌合作推出过**限量版系列产品**。所以从某种意义上说，他最大的商业资产其实就是他的**个人品牌**。

What I admire most is that he combined talent, passion, and **smart business decisions**. He’s still active and respected even in his 50s, and he managed to turn skateboarding into a **global brand** and **business empire**.
我最佩服他的地方是，他把天赋、热爱和**明智的商业决策**结合在了一起。即使到了五十多岁，他仍然很活跃，也依然受到尊重。而且他成功地把滑板变成了一个**全球品牌**和**商业帝国**。

I find that really inspiring because it shows how passion can **grow into something much bigger**.
我觉得这一点非常有启发性，因为它说明，热爱真的可以慢慢发展成一件更大的事业。

<script>
(function () {
  let accent = "us";
  let currentAudio = null;

  function getYoudaoUrl(text) {
    const type = accent === "uk" ? "1" : "0";
    return "https://dict.youdao.com/dictvoice?type=" + type + "&audio=" + encodeURIComponent(text);
  }

  function fallbackSpeak(text) {
    if (!("speechSynthesis" in window)) return;

    window.speechSynthesis.cancel();

    const utterance = new SpeechSynthesisUtterance(text);
    utterance.lang = accent === "uk" ? "en-GB" : "en-US";
    utterance.rate = 0.85;
    window.speechSynthesis.speak(utterance);
  }

  function speak(text) {
    if (!text) return;

    if (currentAudio) {
      currentAudio.pause();
      currentAudio.currentTime = 0;
    }

    currentAudio = new Audio(getYoudaoUrl(text));
    currentAudio.addEventListener("error", function () {
      fallbackSpeak(text);
    });

    currentAudio.play().catch(function () {
      fallbackSpeak(text);
    });
  }

  document.addEventListener("click", function (event) {
    const accentButton = event.target.closest(".accent-button");
    if (accentButton) {
      accent = accentButton.dataset.accent === "uk" ? "uk" : "us";
      document.querySelectorAll(".accent-button").forEach(function (button) {
        button.classList.remove("active");
      });
      accentButton.classList.add("active");
      return;
    }

    const wordButton = event.target.closest(".speak-word");
    if (wordButton) {
      speak(wordButton.dataset.word || wordButton.textContent.trim());
    }
  });
})();
</script>
