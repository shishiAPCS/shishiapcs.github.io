# Describe a long-term goal you would like to achieve

**You should say:**

* How long you have had this goal
* What the goal is
* How you will achieve it
* And explain why you set this goal

---

# Becoming a Better Skateboarder

# 成为更好的滑板手

---

## A. Story Snapshot

## 故事概览

**One-line summary:**
I want to become a much better skateboarder because skateboarding has been a childhood dream and a symbol of persistence for me.
我想成为更好的滑板手，因为滑板一直是我的童年梦想，也代表着坚持和不断进步。

**Best for these topics:**

* a long-term goal
* a childhood dream
* a sport you want to improve at
* something difficult you want to learn
* a person who inspired you
* a personal challenge
* a goal related to health or fitness

**Emotion path:**
interested → inspired → hopeful → realistic → determined → reflective

---

## B. Story Skeleton

## 故事骨架

**Goal:** become a much better skateboarder, maybe semi-professional one day
（目标：成为更好的滑板手，甚至有一天达到半职业水平）

**How long:** a few years, but interest started in childhood
（持续时间：有这个目标几年了，但兴趣从小时候就开始了）

**Inspiration:** Tony Hawk’s games and videos
（灵感来源：Tony Hawk 的游戏和视频）

**Reason for hope:** Tony Hawk still skated well in his 50s
（希望来源：Tony Hawk 五十多岁仍然滑得很好）

**How to achieve it:** regular practice, tutorials, better skaters, live events
（实现方式：定期练习、看教程、向高手学习、看现场比赛）

**Extra preparation:** improve strength, flexibility, and balance
（额外准备：提升力量、柔韧性和平衡能力）

**Deeper meaning:** keep improving and don’t give up easily
（深层意义：不断进步，不轻易放弃）

## E. Reference Version

## 精讲参考范文

One long-term goal I’d really like to achieve is to become **a much better skateboarder**, maybe even good enough to **reach a semi-professional level** one day.
我很想实现的一个长期目标，是成为一个**更好的滑板手**，甚至希望有一天能达到**半职业水平**。

I’ve had this goal for a few years now, but honestly, the interest started much earlier. When I was a kid, I played the **Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater** games, and that was probably the first time I **became fascinated by skateboarding**.
我有这个目标已经几年了，但说实话，我对滑板的兴趣其实更早就开始了。小时候我玩过 **《Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater》** 这个游戏系列，那可能是我第一次**对滑板产生强烈兴趣**。

Later, when I started watching Tony Hawk’s videos online, I was even more inspired. He was still skating **at such a high level** in his 50s, so I thought, well, I’m still in my 30s, so maybe **it’s not too late for me**.
后来，当我开始在网上看 Tony Hawk 的视频时，我更受鼓舞了。他五十多岁的时候仍然能保持**那么高的滑板水平**，所以我就想，我现在还在三十多岁，也许**对我来说还不算太晚**。

To achieve this goal, I know I need to be **very consistent**. I would need to practice the basic moves regularly, watch tutorials, learn from better skaters, and slowly **build up my skills**.
为了实现这个目标，我知道自己需要**非常坚持**。我需要定期练习基础动作，看教学视频，向更厉害的滑板手学习，然后慢慢**提升自己的技术**。

I’d also love to go to live skateboarding events, because watching professional skaters **perform in person** would probably teach me a lot. You can notice their balance, timing, and confidence in a way that’s hard to see on video.
我也很想去现场看滑板比赛或表演，因为亲眼看职业滑板手**现场表演**，应该会让我学到很多东西。你可以更清楚地观察到他们的平衡、时机和自信，而这些东西在视频里不一定看得那么明显。

Besides skating itself, I’d also need to **work on my fitness**. Skateboarding requires **strength, flexibility, and good balance**, so jogging, running, and doing some basic strength training would all help.
除了滑板本身，我还需要**提升体能**。滑板需要**力量、柔韧性和良好的平衡能力**，所以慢跑、跑步，还有一些基础力量训练都会有帮助。

The main reason I set this goal is that skateboarding has been **a childhood dream of mine**. Tony Hawk has always felt like **a hero to me**. The more I learned about him, the more I admired his **persistence and passion**.
我设定这个目标的主要原因是，滑板一直是**我的一个童年梦想**。Tony Hawk 对我来说一直像是**一个英雄**。我越了解他，就越佩服他的**坚持和热爱**。

So for me, this goal is **not just about learning tricks**. It’s also about becoming the kind of person who **keeps improving and doesn’t give up easily**.
所以对我来说，这个目标**不只是学习滑板动作**。它也代表着我想成为那种**不断进步、不轻易放弃的人**。
